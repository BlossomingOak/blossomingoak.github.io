Soda springs is named after a beautiful natural hot spring located 30 miles south of the interstate. No, this hot spring does not spout Dr. Pepper or Mtn Dew, it is purely Diet Coke. There is a Coca-Cola factory 3 miles away, and some people believe that this soda spring was made due to illegal waste dumpage. That is simply not true.
