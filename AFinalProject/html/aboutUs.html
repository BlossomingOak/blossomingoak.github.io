About Us.

What started as a couple of brothers with three ATV’s has quickly turned into Cozumel’s number one vehicle rental service with two locations, four full-time employees, and over 100 vehicles in inventory. We offer locals, tourists, enthusiasts, and cruise-based touring groups the thrill of a lifetime as they explore the beauty of Cozumel Island.
What has made us the number one rental service are our low prices and easy to use online scheduling system. Your ride can be ready and waiting for you by the time you arrive at our office. Schedule your ride and come meet the crew!
