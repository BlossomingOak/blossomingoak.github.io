Site Name: Harrison's Wonderful Weather

Domain: www.harrisonswonderfulweather.com Price: $1.00 / month (Godaddy.com)

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to deliver news and weather reports for people living in Preston, Soda Springs, Fish Haven, and Storm Center. It will also contain a gallery of images that provide new insights to weather and weather-related patterns in these areas. The mission of this website is to help consumers better understand the climate in which they live.
