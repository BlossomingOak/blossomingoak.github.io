Visual Hierarchy


Hulu
Hulu.com

Hulu utilizes visual hierarchy to first show the message “All your TV in one place”, then a brief description with a call to action, and then finally their logo.

Proximity


Bing
Bing.com

Bing uses proximity between their logo and the search bar, while creating a lack of proximity (distance) from other aspects of the page such as news articles. It is clear that their main purpose is to provide a search engine, and they use proximity to illustrate that.

Rule of Thirds


Wikipedia
Wikipedia.com

Wikipedia uses the rule of thirds to emphasize their search bar and languages, and de-emphasize the other front page content.
